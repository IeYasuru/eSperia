All you have to do is extract it on your server.
It contains costume.txt sample script.

to use this plugins you must add it in plugin.conf

go to conf/plugins.conf

at the bottom search for 

plugins_list: [
	/* Enable HPMHooking when plugins in use rely on Hooking */
	"HPMHooking",
	"costumeitem",
	//"db2sql",
	//"sample",
	//"other",
]

uncomment HPMHooking
and add costumeitem.
